# Pivot Table Architecture

Multiple pivot tables were created to support analysis:

## Executive KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Profit Margin

## Revenue Trend
- Monthly and yearly revenue patterns
- Identifies seasonality and growth trends

## Product Performance
- Revenue and profit by category and product
- Highlights top-performing products

## Customer Segmentation
- Revenue and profit by age group and gender
- Identifies high-value customer segments

## Geographic Analysis
- Revenue by country and state
- Identifies regional performance differences

## Profitability Analysis
- Compares revenue and profit margin by category
